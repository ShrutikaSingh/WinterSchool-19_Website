Motivation of WINTER SCHOOL

Welcome to the Winter School'19

Here, at MIT-WPU, we consistently strive to extend the horizons of Computer Engineering and Technology to provide our students access to the most advanced technologies being developed every day. Through the Winter School, it is now possible for 50 dedicated and talented students all over India to engage and experiment with that we have to offer: from visionary speakers to state of art hardware. Aspiring to open the boundaries of imagination for an out of the box education, this venture moves hand in hand with fundamental Computer Science concepts to strengthen our future tech-makers.
