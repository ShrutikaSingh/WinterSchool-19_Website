AR, VR, Mixed Reality

Augmented reality (AR) adds digital elements to a live view often by using the camera on a smartphone like Snapchat lenses and the game Pokemon Go. Virtual reality (VR) implies a complete immersion experience that shuts out the physical world.

HCI / BCI

Tridimensional representations stimulate cognitive processes that are foundation of human-computer interaction (HCI). BCI is a nonmuscular communication channel that attempts to reestablish the interaction between an individual & environment.

Robotics

Robotics is an interdisciplinary branch of engineering that includes mechanical, computer science, electronic engineering, and others. Robotics deals with the design, construction, operation, as well as computer systems for their control, sensory feedback, and information processing.

Drones

Drones are more formally known as unmanned aerial vehicles (UAVs) or unmanned aircraft systems (UASes). Essentially, a drone is a flying robot that can be remotely controlled or fly autonomously through software-controlled flight plans in their embedded systems, working in conjunction with onboard sensors and GPS.

Music Programming
January 14, 2019 Laura Green

Music programming is a common element of modern music production & mixing. The term “programming” in this context can refer to creating automated changes in audio effects such as dynamic EQ and compression, to the use of a step sequencer to create beats and music.

Fundamental Computer Science

Fundamental Concept. A computer is basically a programmable machine capable to perform arithmetic and logical operations automatically and sequentially. It is also known as a data processor, as it can store, process, and retrieve data as per the wish of the user with the helpof softwares.
